# Courses

This section contains structured learning materials, notes, exercises, and progress related to digital marketing courses and certifications.

It is used to document practical learning from platforms, training programs, and certification paths.

---

## Purpose

- Track course progress and learning outcomes  
- Organize certification-related notes and exercises  
- Reinforce understanding through structured documentation  
- Build practical knowledge from guided learning paths  

---

## Topics

Examples include:

- Digital marketing fundamentals  
- Content marketing  
- Email marketing  
- SEO concepts  
- Marketing analytics  
- CRM and customer engagement systems  

---

## Approach

Course learning is documented with practical understanding in mind rather than memorization.

The focus is on:
- concepts
- workflows
- tools
- operational understanding
- practical application

---

## Goal

To build a strong foundation in digital marketing through structured and continuous learning.
