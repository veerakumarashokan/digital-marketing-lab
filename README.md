# Digital Marketing Lab

This repository focuses on practical digital marketing learning through hands-on workflows, marketing operations, analytics, and business-oriented experimentation.

It is designed to document learning progress, tools, campaigns, observations, and real-world marketing concepts used in modern digital environments.

---

## Purpose

- Build practical digital marketing knowledge  
- Understand marketing workflows and operational systems  
- Learn analytics, campaign structures, and customer engagement concepts  
- Document hands-on learning and practical experimentation  

---

## Focus Areas

- Marketing fundamentals  
- Campaign workflows and operations  
- Content systems and engagement strategies  
- Analytics and reporting concepts  
- Business and customer-oriented processes  

---

## Structure

- `courses/` → Certification and course-related learning  
- `notes/` → Concepts, strategies, and observations  
- `journal/` → Progress tracking and practical experiences  
- `projects/` → Practical implementations and experiments  

---

## Approach

This repository emphasizes practical understanding and consistent learning through documentation, experimentation, and operational thinking.

---

## Goal

To build strong practical foundations in digital marketing while developing transferable operational and analytical skills.
